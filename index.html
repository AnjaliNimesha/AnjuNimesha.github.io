Our Designs

Wall Art

Resin is a fabulous medium to use to make wall hanging art.The materials i used to make wall arts are 32 oz.Envirotex Lite resin. Opaque pigments, Transparent dyes and acrylic paints,canvas.

Resin ceramic

Exopxy resin is completely neutral once int has had a chance to cure completely.We can use any epoxy resin that has been designed for sealing and surface protection to coat cuttinf boards,plates,or decorative tableware.There is no risk of food contamination once the epoxy has cured completely.

Jewelery

We are making resin jewelery by pouring resin into a mold (made of plastic or silicone)or into a material pendant frame called a bezel.The resin is cure permanently to the shape of the mold or bezel and we are emblishing it with colorant or inclusions to give it a one-of-a kind look.

Novelty Accessories

Novelty accessories are one of the best ways for you to create something amazing with resin that you can use in your everyday life. These can be anything from keychains, to pendants, to book covers, to smartphone covers, and even pen holders as Accessories.

Artwork

As we mentioned previously, art is something that’s made most commonly with resin, but the sheer variety of art you’re able to make with resin is astounding. A crowd favorite is resin cast figurines which can be painted and coated in near-infinite variations. Resin-coated paintings and even mixed media resin artwork are really common these days, with impressive displays of ingenuity and completely original thinking all around.
